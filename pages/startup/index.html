About

Analytics Entrepreneurship at LOYNO

The Peter Callais Institute Tech Startup Program supports students and recent graduates who want to build analytics-driven companies. Whether you're building a SaaS tool, a data marketplace, an AI application, or an analytics consulting practice, this program gives you the structure, mentorship, and resources to move from idea to launch.

The program runs in two tracks: a semester-long Incubator Track for early-stage ideas, and a year-long Accelerator Track for ventures with initial traction seeking their first customers or investment.

All participants receive access to cloud computing credits (AWS, GCP, Azure), software licenses, co-working space at the Institute, and a dedicated mentor from the Institute's industry advisory network.

INCUBATOR TRACK

1 semester · Idea to MVP · Open to all students

ACCELERATOR TRACK

1 year · MVP to market · By application only
