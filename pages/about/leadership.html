Founding Director

Dr. Nuwan Indika, Ph.D.

J. William Fulbright Scholar | Chase / Francis C. Doyle Distinguished Professor

Dr. Nuwan Indika is the founding director of the Peter Callais Institute for Business Analytics at Loyola University New Orleans. A J. William Fulbright Scholar, he holds the Chase/Francis C. Doyle Distinguished Professorship and serves as Assistant Professor in the College of Business.

His research spans machine learning, econometrics, statistical modeling, and applied business analytics. He has published in leading academic journals and presented at major international conferences on topics including AI fairness, predictive modeling, and data-driven decision-making in business contexts.

Prior to Loyola, Dr. Indika held research positions at leading institutions in the United States and internationally. He is deeply committed to Jesuit values of education, service, and justice — and brings this commitment to every aspect of the Institute's work.
