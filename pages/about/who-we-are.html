In Memoriam

About Peter Callais

In 2025, the Callais Family established the Peter Callais Institute for Business Analytics to honor the memory of Peter Callais, a devoted leader, entrepreneur, and family man. A native of Golden Meadow and a resident of Cut Off, Louisiana, Peter graduated from Tulane's Freeman School of Business in 1986 and served as CEO of Abdon Callais Offshore, guiding the company to national recognition.

Beyond business, he was deeply committed to his faith and community, teaching Religion in Golden Meadow, serving as a Fourth Degree Knight of Columbus, and holding leadership roles in various organizations, including Community Bank and SWDI. His contributions earned him numerous accolades, including Louisiana's Small Business Person of the Year and Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year.

An avid outdoorsman, Peter loved scuba diving, hunting, and fishing. His legacy of innovation, service, and passion lives on through the institute, inspiring future generations.

Our City

Analytics in the Crescent City

New Orleans is one of the most analytically fascinating cities in America. Its unique economic landscape — shaped by tourism, energy, healthcare, port logistics, and a thriving creative economy — generates the kind of complex, multi-dimensional data challenges that make for compelling analytics work.

From Hurricane Katrina recovery data to food desert mapping to predictive policing debates, New Orleans offers our students real problems with real stakes. We believe analytics education is most powerful when it's grounded in place.

  • ✓ Home to a major U.S. port — logistics & supply chain data
  • ✓ Energy sector hub — oil, gas, and renewables analytics
  • ✓ Major medical center and healthcare innovation corridor
  • ✓ Rich nonprofit and social impact ecosystem
