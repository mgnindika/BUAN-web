Hiring Our Researchers

The Peter Callais Institute is proud to present our doctoral research fellows and postdoctoral researchers entering the job market this year. These talented individuals are available for faculty positions, research scientist roles, and senior analytics leadership positions. For placement inquiries, contact Dr. Nuwan Indika at nindika@loyno.edu.

Julian Arceneaux

Academic Market

Doctoral Research Fellow · Dissertation: "Reinforcement Learning for Dynamic Portfolio Optimization"

Julian's dissertation develops novel reinforcement learning algorithms for portfolio optimization under non-Gaussian return distributions. His work bridges quantitative finance and deep learning, with applied contributions to options pricing and tail risk management. He will be defending in May 2025 and is seeking tenure-track positions in Finance, Quantitative Finance, or Business Analytics.

Research Areas
