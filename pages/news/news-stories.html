Callais Institute Receives $2.1M NIH Grant for Healthcare AI Research

March 12, 2025 | By Institute Staff

The National Institutes of Health has awarded a $2.1 million R01 grant to Dr. Nuwan Indika and the Callais Institute to develop interpretable machine learning tools for clinical sepsis prediction in under-resourced hospitals across Louisiana and Mississippi. The three-year project will deploy and evaluate AI-assisted early warning systems in five rural hospitals beginning in Fall 2025.
