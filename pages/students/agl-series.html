About the Series

Learning from Those Doing the Work

The Analytics Guest Lecture Series (AGL) brings accomplished data professionals to campus each semester to share real-world insights, career wisdom, and emerging trends directly with students and faculty.

Sessions span industries including healthcare, finance, government, energy, retail, and nonprofits — reflecting the diversity of analytics applications. Most sessions are hybrid (in-person + Zoom), and recordings are archived for later viewing.

Lectures are free and open to all Loyola students and faculty. Eligible for co-curricular credit in select programs.
