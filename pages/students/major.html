Program Overview

Why Business Analytics?

Business Analytics is one of the fastest-growing fields in the global economy. Organizations of every size and sector need professionals who can collect, analyze, and communicate data-driven insights — and act on them ethically and effectively.

Loyola University New Orleans offers a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) with a major in Business Analytics through the AACSB-accredited College of Business. The program uniquely combines quantitative rigor with Jesuit ethical reasoning — producing analysts who are both technically excellent and morally grounded.

The major is available as a primary degree or as a double major, making it an excellent complement to Finance, Marketing, Computer Science, Economics, Management, and more.
