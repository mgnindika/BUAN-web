About the Event

What Is Impact Day?

Impact Day is the Callais Institute's flagship annual event — held at the end of the Spring semester. It brings together students, faculty, industry partners, community members, and media to celebrate a year's worth of analytics work.

From poster sessions and panel discussions to keynote speakers and awards, Impact Day is where the Callais Institute community comes together to reflect, celebrate, and connect.
