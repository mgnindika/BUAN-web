Focus Areas

Research Themes

The Institute's research is organized around six applied themes — each connecting data science methods to real-world domains.

🤖

Machine Learning & AI

Deep learning, interpretable AI, fairness and bias mitigation, and responsible deployment of ML systems in business and public sector contexts.

🏥

Health Analytics

Clinical NLP, EHR data mining, readmission prediction, health equity measurement, and AI for clinical decision support.

📈

Finance & Risk

Credit risk, fraud detection, algorithmic trading, portfolio optimization, and financial time series forecasting.

🌊

Environmental Analytics

Coastal resilience, wetlands monitoring, climate adaptation modeling, and remote sensing analysis for Louisiana ecosystems.

🌍

Social Impact & Equity

Housing, food access, educational equity, criminal justice, and community development analytics rooted in justice-oriented methodologies.

🏢

Business & Operations

Supply chain optimization, demand forecasting, marketing analytics, customer behavior modeling, and operational efficiency.
