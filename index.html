A Message from Our Director

Building the Future of Analytics at Loyola

"At the Callais Institute, we believe data is not just numbers — it is the language of impact. Our work is grounded in Loyola's Jesuit commitment to justice, and powered by the analytical tools of tomorrow."

The Peter Callais Institute for Business Analytics was established in 2025 through a transformational gift from the Callais Family, honoring the memory of Peter Callais — entrepreneur, community leader, and visionary.

We are building something extraordinary here in New Orleans: a hub where rigorous analytics education meets Jesuit values of service, ethics, and justice. Our students don't just analyze data — they use it to improve lives.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

"

The Peter Callais Institute for Business Analytics at Loyola University New Orleans is committed to honoring Peter Callais' legacy of leadership, innovation, and service. We educate and empower students, professionals, and organizations through rigorous analytics training, interdisciplinary collaboration, and industry partnerships. Guided by Jesuit values, we cultivate ethical, innovative, and socially responsible leaders who use the power of data to advance business, strengthen communities, and inspire positive change.

Our Namesake

In Honor of Peter Callais

In 2025, the Callais Family established this institute to honor the memory of Peter Callais — a devoted leader, entrepreneur, and family man. A Tulane Freeman School graduate and CEO of Abdon Callais Offshore, Peter guided his company to national recognition.

His legacy of innovation, service, and passion lives on through the institute — inspiring the next generation of ethical data-driven leaders.
